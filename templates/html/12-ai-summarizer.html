Scaling Language Models: Methods, Analysis & Insights from Training Gopher

DeepMind · Published May 2024 · 18,420 words

Language model performance has been consistently improving with scale, motivating interest in training very large models. However, the relationship between model size, dataset size, and compute budget for optimal performance is poorly understood. In this paper, we investigate the scaling behavior of language model performance on a diverse set of tasks...

We train a series of language models ranging from 44M to 280B parameters, named Gopher, on a high-quality dataset. We analyze model performance across 152 diverse tasks, finding that larger models are often better, but improvements depend heavily on the task category...
