Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately and billing is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

How does the annual billing discount work?

When you choose annual billing, you save 20% compared to the monthly rate. You're charged once per year for the full annual amount, and no refunds are issued for unused months.

What happens if I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel at any time. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. We don't offer refunds for partial months on monthly plans.
